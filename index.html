About Us

A Presbyterian church in the historic Reformed tradition

Philadelphia Presbyterian Church is a preaching station of the Free Church of Scotland (Continuing). We seek to glorify God through the preaching of His Word, prayer, the singing of Psalms, and the faithful administration of Christ's ordinances.

Our desire is to see sinners brought to Christ, believers strengthened in grace, and families built up in the fear and admonition of the Lord.

What We Believe

Our doctrine is summarized in the Westminster Standards.

Scripture

The Holy Scriptures are the Word of God and the only infallible rule of faith and life.

Christ

Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God, the only Mediator between God and man, and the only Saviour of sinners.

Worship

God alone determines how He is to be worshipped. We therefore seek to worship Him according to His revealed will.

Psalms

We sing the inspired Psalms of Scripture without instrumental accompaniment in public worship.

Sabbath

We believe the Lord's Day is the Christian Sabbath, set apart for worship, mercy, and holy rest.

Covenant Life

We believe God deals with His people covenantally, calling believers and their households to walk before Him.

Worship

What to expect when you visit

Our worship is simple, reverent, and centered upon the ordinary means of grace. The service includes the reading and preaching of Scripture, prayer, and the singing of Psalms.

We encourage visitors to come as they are able and to ask any questions they may have after the service.
